Tinder uses a lot of red to represent love in their application,
this works because in the west, red is typically associated with
love and valentines day. In china purple is more commonly associated
with love, in order to attract the local chinese market we could change
the general color scheme to purple. When affirming you want to date
someone in tinder you click a green heart. In chinese culture, green
doesnt represent love so a color shift would help with localisation.
Before releasing it would be helpful to test what colors best represent
love or affirmation of love to the chinese population using the app.
