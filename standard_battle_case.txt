One prominent battle between files types in the tech world
is that between .zip compression and .tar.gz compression.
Overall, .zip is a much more commonly used archive file.
One of the main reason for this is the prominence of windows,
.zip works a lot better for cross platform transfer, especially
windows. Since windows is by far the most popular desktop
OS, the convenience of .zip won over most people. For a class
project i would use .tar.gz because its the standard for linux,
the kernel used here in the lab. I also use linux at home so
that makes it more convenient.
